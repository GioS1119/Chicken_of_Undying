# 3D Chicken of Undying
Transform your Totem of Undying into a 3D chicken that will be with you forever! ..._until death do you part_

![Showcase of the resource pack: a player in 2nd person view holding a Chicken of Undying](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/568f3668333a4dcd8ee055cc1ed1d65b8013f3f9.png)

This resource pack replaces the standard totem with Minecraft's beloved chicken. This pack features custom poses for all various scenarios ([_see gallery_](https://modrinth.com/resourcepack/chickenofundying/gallery)). In 2nd and 3rd person views, your chicken will be perched on your shoulder.
The old, boring "Totem of Undying" name is replaced with "Chicken of Undying", also available in various languages.

![The new names of the "Totem of Undying" in English, Italian, Portuguese and Upside down English](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/14837e00b6872778e5db956f007e45e72fea04eb.png)

You can also enable a 2D texture with [Respackopts](https://modrinth.com/mod/respackopts). Which by default is enabled only for GUIs.

There are even some easter eggs, try to find them!

In the future, I want to add animations with the [Punchy!](https://modrinth.com/mod/punchy-fpa) mod, but for now, this is just an idea.
